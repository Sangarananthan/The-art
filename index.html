7 TYPES OF ART

Art spans visual, literary, performing, music, film, architecture, and digital realms. Visual art includes painting, sculpture, and printmaking; literary art encompasses novels, poems, and essays. Performing arts comprise theater, dance, and music. Film combines visual storytelling. Architecture designs buildings. Digital art utilizes technology for creative expression, reflecting modern sensibilities.
